What to get ready before you sign up

Make sure you have gathered feedback from different decision makers in your organisation. You should speak to your IT manager for technical input. Also, ensure you have come to an agreement with your financial and business lead before installing GovWifi.

What the setup process will look like

We have a step by step process that will run you through the set up. We will provide recommendations and further advice along the way, including how to:

  • add the IP address
  • choose the technical infrastructure
  • set up a support model

Running a trial

You must provide a suitable amount of internet bandwidth for the anticipated number of users. Plan for 50% of the AP vendor’s recommended client device count per AP radio. Stay well under the vendor’s published maximum figure (for example, one access point per block of 20 users, located in the middle of each group).
