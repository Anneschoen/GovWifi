Get started

What to know beforehand

You will have a range of options to monitor and control the performance of the network - however, you should make sure that this model is supported by the people that manage the IT team as well as senior decision makers.

We want to make it easy for government organisations to use GovWifi. Follow these instructions to:

  • get started having conversations about GovWifi within your organisation
  • set up the GovWifi service on your infrastructure
  • set up managed end user devices to automatically connect to GovWifi
